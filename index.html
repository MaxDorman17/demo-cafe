Our Story

A Café That Feels Like Home

The Harbour Café has been a fixture on Kirkcaldy's waterfront since 2014. We started with a passion for good coffee and honest food — and that hasn't changed a bit.

Everything on our menu is made fresh in-house every morning. Our coffee is roasted locally, our bread is baked daily, and our soups are made from scratch. No shortcuts, no frozen food, no fuss.
